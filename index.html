How to Choose Your Engineering Discipline

  • Mechatronics: Perfect if you're into robotics, automation, embedded systems, and blending hardware with software.
  • Electrical: Great for diving deep into circuits, power systems, and electronics design.
  • Mechanical: Ideal if you enjoy thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, machine design, and materials.
